About Us

Connecting Australian resources with the world

Our Business

DZ Commodities is an Australian-based commodity trading company specializing in the export of thermal and coking coal. We connect Australian mining operations with international markets across Asia and Europe.

Our focus is on reliable sourcing, quality assurance, and building long-term relationships with both suppliers and buyers. We understand the complexities of international commodity trading and provide seamless solutions for our partners.

Our Values

Reliability

We deliver on our commitments with consistent supply and transparent communication throughout the trading process.

Quality

We maintain strict quality control standards to ensure all products meet agreed specifications.

Integrity

Honest and ethical business practices form the foundation of all our relationships.

Partnership

We view our clients as long-term partners and work collaboratively to achieve mutual success.
